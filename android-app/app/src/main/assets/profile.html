About Maria

I specialize in authentic Iberian cuisine using fresh, locally-sourced ingredients from La Boqueria and local Madrid markets. Every dish I prepare is made with love and a commitment to preserving traditional Spanish culinary techniques while adding my own creative flair. 🥘🍷
